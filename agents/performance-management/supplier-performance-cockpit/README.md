# Supplier Performance Cockpit

The **Supplier Performance Cockpit** agent is about **monitoring and insight**: it turns KPI streams into **plain-language narratives**, **anomaly callouts**, and **exportable** views that power QBRs and exec snapshots.

## Why it matters

Leaders do not lack charts—they lack **coherent stories** that connect KPI movement to **operational implications**. This agent bridges BI tiles and human decisions.

## Common inputs

- Time-series KPIs by supplier/site/category
- Targets and thresholds
- Optional qualitative notes (survey, major incidents)

## Typical outputs

- “What changed / why it matters” storyline
- Ranked anomalies with suggested drill paths (not accusations)
- CSV/BI export spec for slide automation

## Workflow in practice

Weekly **pulse** reviews and pre-QBR **executive snapshots**. Hand serious governance actions to **Supplier Performance Management**.

## Extension opportunities

- Statistical process control rules (Western Electric rules) behind anomaly detection
- Natural language explanations layered on deterministic alerts

## Differentiation

- **SPM** drives **actions, cadence, and escalations**.
- **Cockpit** drives **visibility, monitoring, and performance insight**.
