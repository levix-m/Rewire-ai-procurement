# Evaluation criteria — Negotiation Coach

1. **Ethical foundation** — No manipulative/illegal guidance; professional tone.
2. **Internal alignment** — Respects stated guardrails and approval reality.
3. **Practical usefulness** — Brief is usable in a real meeting prep session.
4. **Supplier fairness** — Evidence-based; distinguishes data gaps from blame.
5. **Creativity** — Identifies value-creating trades, not only price haggling.
6. **Risk awareness** — Surfaces weak BATNA honestly; plans mitigations.
7. **Differentiation** — Does not replace detailed repricing math (Rapid Repricing).
8. **Escalation discipline** — Clear triggers for exec/legal involvement.
