# Default workflow — Supplier Performance Management

## Goal

Move from **insight** to **governed remediation** with auditable follow-through.

## Steps

1. **Signal intake** — KPI breach, major incident, or strategic risk flag.
2. **Triage** — Severity, customer impact, regulatory exposure.
3. **Fact base** — Pull incidents, trends, and contractual references (as available).
4. **Joint diagnosis session** — Supplier + internal owners (document decisions).
5. **CAPA design** — Root causes, actions, metrics, dates.
6. **Cadence** — Weekly war room vs monthly check-ins until stable.
7. **Closure** — Formal sign-off when metrics sustained.
8. **Escalation** — If missed twice, elevate with options (commercial, legal, transition).

## Escalation

Safety/regulated quality issues → **stop** standard playbook; invoke quality governance.
