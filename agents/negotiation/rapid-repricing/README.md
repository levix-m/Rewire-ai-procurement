# Rapid Repricing

**Rapid Repricing** supports **fast-moving commercial math** during negotiations: recomputing grids when volumes shift, surcharges toggle, FX moves, or spot indices spike—so teams can respond **in-session** with traceable assumptions.

## Why it matters

Negotiations compress into hours. This agent keeps **repricing logic** organized: what changed, what stayed, and what Finance will accept as a coherent package.

## Common inputs

- Current price lists, tier breaks, rebate mechanics
- Demand shifts or “what-if” volumes
- FX tables, fuel/resin indices, passthrough formulas

## Typical outputs

- Side-by-side scenario tables (base vs counter vs walk)
- Sensitivity of total cost to top 3 drivers
- Assumption log suitable for deal desk review

## Workflow in practice

Use in **summits**, **RFx final rounds**, and **spot buy** escalations. Complements **ICM** for macro drivers and **Negotiation Coach** for strategy.

## Extension opportunities

- Deterministic engine with LLM explaining results in plain language
- Guardrails from Finance: floor/ceiling automation flags

## Differentiation

- **Negotiation Coach** focuses on **what to say and trade**.
- **Rapid Repricing** focuses on **what the numbers do** under time pressure.
