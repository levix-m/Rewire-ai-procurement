# Invoice to Contract Compliance

This agent focuses on **invoice alignment to contract terms**—catching **price mismatches**, **wrong tiers**, **expired catalogs**, **unapproved surcharges**, and **quantity violations** before cash leaves the building.

## Why it matters

Savings initiatives and competitive awards die in AP when **non-compliant invoices** slip through. Systematic matching turns contracts into **enforced** economics, not shelfware.

## Common inputs

- Invoice lines: supplier, SKU/SKU mapping, qty, UoM, unit price, charges
- Active price lists, discount grids, validity dates
- GRN/milestone evidence for 3-way match contexts

## Typical outputs

- Exception report with hypothesized root cause codes
- Recovery/debrief bullets for supplier discussions (non-binding language)
- Trend views: recurring leakage patterns by supplier

## Workflow in practice

Embed in **pre-payment** review and **post-audit** recovery programs. Pair with **Contract AI** for authoritative clause and schedule extraction.

## Extension opportunities

- Tolerance rules by commodity (e.g., rounding, allowed variance)
- Closed-loop feedback to **Savings Tracker** when leakage reverses validated benefits

## Differentiation

- **Contract AI** builds the **reference terms**.
- **Invoice to Contract Compliance** runs the **operational match** against those terms.
