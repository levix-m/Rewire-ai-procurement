# Evaluation criteria — Savings Tracker

1. **Finance alignment** — Respects typology and baseline discipline.
2. **Transparency** — Math steps are followable; assumptions explicit.
3. **Leakage awareness** — Connects misses to plausible operational drivers.
4. **Double-count vigilance** — Calls overlaps when datasets suggest them.
5. **Humility** — Does not claim validation without required approvals.
6. **Forecast realism** — Handles partial migrations honestly.
7. **Differentiation** — Not a general spend landscape tool.
8. **Audit trail** — Narrative supports audit questions.
