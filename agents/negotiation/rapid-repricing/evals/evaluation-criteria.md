# Evaluation criteria — Rapid Repricing

1. **Numerical discipline** — Tier/FX math directionally sound; cliffs flagged.
2. **Transparency** — Assumptions and open fields explicit.
3. **Speed usefulness** — Output structure supports sub-hour decisions.
4. **Finance alignment** — Respects tax basis and rounding instructions.
5. **Sensitivity insight** — Calls out dominant drivers and nonlinearities.
6. **Humility** — Does not fabricate missing mechanical rules.
7. **Differentiation** — Stays out of full negotiation strategy (Negotiation Coach).
8. **Auditability** — Assumption log suitable for deal file.
