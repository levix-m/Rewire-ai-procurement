# Example tasks — Supplier Performance Management

- “Draft a QBR agenda for a tier-1 supplier with three recurring OTD misses.”
- “Create a corrective action plan for quality PPM above threshold for 60 days.”
- “When do we escalate from category lead to steering committee for SLA breaches?”
- “Our tickets show slow responsiveness—how do we separate supplier vs internal delays?”
- “Prepare an escalation memo tying performance to contractual remedies (non-legal).”
