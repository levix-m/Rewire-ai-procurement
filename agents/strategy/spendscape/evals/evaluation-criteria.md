# Evaluation criteria — Spendscape

1. **Numerical integrity** — Totals and shares reconcile to the provided slice; no invented suppliers or spend.
2. **Opportunity plausibility** — Hypotheses match fragmentation/concentration patterns; effort/risk labels are sensible.
3. **Executive clarity** — A CPO or CFO reader grasps the story in the first 90 seconds.
4. **Data honesty** — Blind spots and taxonomy defects are visible, not buried.
5. **Strategic differentiation** — Clearly distinct from “data cleansing” advice (that belongs to SpendSculptor).
6. **Risk awareness** — Flags single-source, regulated, and competition-sensitive contexts appropriately.
7. **Action routing** — Names the right forums/owners for next steps, not generic “stakeholders.”
8. **Consistency** — Same dataset slice yields the same ranked opportunities unless randomness is declared.

**Release gate:** no score below **2** on numerical integrity, data honesty, or risk awareness.
