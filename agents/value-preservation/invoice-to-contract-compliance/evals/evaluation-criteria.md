# Evaluation criteria — Invoice to Contract Compliance

1. **Matching correctness** — Applies schedule + PO rules as given; arithmetic sound.
2. **Classification quality** — Exception codes map to actionable teams.
3. **Exposure estimation** — Quantifies impact transparently; shows formulas.
4. **Professional tone** — Supplier communications are factual, not hostile.
5. **Control mindset** — Suggests durable prevention, not one-off fixes only.
6. **Legal humility** — Avoids legal conclusions on claims.
7. **Differentiation** — Does not perform full contract mapping (Contract AI).
8. **Traceability** — Ties back to specific lines and effective dates.
