# Sample output — Negotiation Coach

## Situation snapshot

Incumbent has **credibility on delivery** but **limited improvement narrative**. Their opening **6%** conflicts with internal **CPI + 0.5%** guardrail without structured trades.

## Objectives

- **Must-have:** OTIF ≥97.5% with measurable remediation plan if missed
- **Must-have:** Year-1 increase within guardrail unless exception package approved pre-meeting
- **Nice-to-have:** Funded continuous improvement backlog (2–3 projects)

## Leverage (fact-based)

- Consolidation scope is valuable to them; use as **get** for disciplined pricing
- Performance gap vs target is objective; avoid personal blame—focus on metrics and incentives

## Trade matrix

| We can give | We want |
|-------------|---------|
| Extend contract term to 3 years with mutual exit for sustained misses | Cap year-1 increase at CPI + 0.5% + performance credits |
| Consolidate two secondary lanes | Funded Kaizen projects with shared savings split |

## Conversation plan

- Open with **joint success metrics** for launches; anchor on **partnership governance**
- Probe cost structure at **category level** (labor, fuel, productivity)—not accusatory
- If stuck, pivot to **scenario workshop** (volumes, lanes, service scope)

## Escalation triggers

- Supplier refuses any performance remedy tied to credits → involve ops sponsor + exec alignment
- Request exceeds guardrail without pre-approved exception → pause for CFO decision

## Debrief prompts

- Which trades landed? What data changed their position? What will we document in SRM?
