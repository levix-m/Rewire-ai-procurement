# Savings Tracker

**Savings Tracker** is the **VMO agent** for **benefits realization**: it follows initiatives from **idea → baseline → execution → validated savings**, aligned to **finance rules** that define what counts.

## Why it matters

Procurement credibility lives in **validated numbers**. This agent helps teams avoid **double counting**, **baseline games**, and **mystery leakage** when actuals arrive.

## Common inputs

- Initiative records: owner, category, effective date, hypothesis
- Baselines approved by Finance
- Actual pricing/volume after implementation

## Typical outputs

- Portfolio dashboard narrative: on track, at risk, closed
- Initiative validation worksheet with transparent math
- Leakage diagnostics when realized savings miss plan

## Workflow in practice

Monthly **VMO** cadence; tight coupling with **FP&A** during forecasting cycles.

## Extension opportunities

- Automated reconciliation to **invoice-level** price compliance (see Invoice to Contract Compliance)
- Risk flags when **Spend Control Tower** shows maverick erosion post-award

## Differentiation

- **Spend Control Tower** focuses on **process compliance**.
- **Savings Tracker** focuses on **financial validation of value delivered**.
