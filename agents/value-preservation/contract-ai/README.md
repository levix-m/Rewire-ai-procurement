# Contract AI

**Contract AI** supports **contract intelligence** across the portfolio: surfacing **obligations**, **renewal risks**, and **non-standard clauses** with **citations**—so procurement and legal ops can prioritize reviews.

## Why it matters

Value leaks when nobody remembers which agreement allows **auto-renewal**, which caps **liability**, or which **price holds** expire mid-quarter. This agent accelerates **structured understanding** without replacing attorneys.

## Common inputs

- Executed agreements and amendments stored in CLM
- Clause taxonomy: pricing, term, termination, liability, data, SLAs
- Renewal parameters and notice windows

## Typical outputs

- Clause map: category of clause → citation snippet reference
- Draft renewal calendar entries (for human confirmation)
- Risk heat notes: non-standard vs playbook

## Workflow in practice

Use during **intake**, **renewal planning**, and **M&A harmonization** backlogs.

**Important:** Outputs are **hypotheses** until Legal confirms—design workflows accordingly.

## Extension opportunities

- Feed structured price terms into **Invoice to Contract Compliance** matchers
- Link obligations to **Supplier Performance Management** KPIs

## Differentiation

- **Contract AI** understands **agreement text and structure**.
- **Invoice to Contract Compliance** checks **operational transactions** against terms.
