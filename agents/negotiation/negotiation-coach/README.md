# Negotiation Coach

The **Negotiation Coach** helps procurement professionals prepare **high-stakes conversations**: clear objectives, defensible positions, **tradeable concessions**, and **escalation paths** that stay aligned to business strategy.

## Why it matters

Negotiation outcomes hinge on preparation more than charisma. This agent structures **facts, limits, and psychology** into a practical brief—without replacing human judgment on relationship and context.

## Common inputs

- Current commercial terms, volume levers, and competitive alternatives
- Supplier performance narrative (quality, delivery, responsiveness)
- Internal guardrails: margin floors, legal non-negotiables, exec involvement triggers

## Typical outputs

- Opening–target–walk-away framework by negotiation workstream
- Concession sequencing with **give/get** logic
- Debrief template to institutionalize learning

## Workflow in practice

Use before **renewals**, **RFx final rounds**, and **escalations**. Pair with **Rapid Repricing** when numeric scenarios must be recomputed live.

## Extension opportunities

- Pull KPI snapshots from **Supplier Performance Cockpit** automatically
- Link to **Contract AI** for clause-aware flex points (non-legal)

## Differentiation

- **Rapid Repricing** computes **numbers fast**.
- **Negotiation Coach** designs the **strategy and conversation**.
