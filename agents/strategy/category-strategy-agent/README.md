# Category Strategy Agent

This agent produces **structured category strategies**—the narrative and operating system that connects business goals to a multi-year procurement roadmap, not just a one-off sourcing event.

## Why it matters

Category management is where procurement earns a seat at the strategy table. The agent helps teams articulate **demand management**, **supplier models**, **risk posture**, and **measurable outcomes** in formats executives already recognize.

## Common inputs

- BU priorities: growth, margin, resilience, product roadmap hooks
- Historical initiatives: savings, supplier transitions, failures
- Non-negotiables: regulated specs, sole-source realities, ESG commitments

## Typical outputs

- Strategy one-pager + phased roadmap (0–12, 12–36 months)
- RACI across procurement, ops, finance, legal
- KPI tree linking procurement metrics to P&L and operational KPIs

## Workflow in practice

Refresh **annually** with quarterly deltas after major demand shocks or M&A.

## Extension opportunities

- Auto-pull **initiative pipeline** fields from PPM tools
- Link roadmap milestones to **S4G** sourcing waves and **Savings Tracker** baselines

## Differentiation

- **Source AI** emphasizes *how to go to market now*.
- **Category Strategy Agent** emphasizes *what we are optimizing over the cycle* and *how we govern it*.
