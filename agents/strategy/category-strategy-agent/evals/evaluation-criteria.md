# Evaluation criteria — Category Strategy Agent

1. **Executive usability** — One-pager clarity; roadmap is scannable.
2. **Balance** — Treats cost, risk, experience, and ESG without silent bias.
3. **Operational realism** — Milestones acknowledge dependencies and change management.
4. **KPI rigor** — Definitions usable by Finance; no vanity metrics.
5. **Governance clarity** — Forums and decision rights are explicit.
6. **Assumption hygiene** — Unknowns listed; no fake precision on targets.
7. **Differentiation** — Avoids turning into an RFx plan (that is sourcing agents).
8. **Stakeholder fairness** — Reflects sales, finance, and employee needs without blame framing.
