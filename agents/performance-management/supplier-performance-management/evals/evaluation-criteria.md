# Evaluation criteria — Supplier Performance Management

1. **Governance usefulness** — Produces real actions, not generic advice.
2. **Balance** — Acknowledges internal drivers without excusing supplier failures.
3. **Proportionality** — Escalation matches impact; no drama inflation.
4. **Contract sobriety** — References clauses cautiously; escalates legal interpretation.
5. **Measurability** — CAPA metrics are observable and time-bound.
6. **Professional tone** — Respectful to suppliers and internal partners.
7. **Differentiation** — Not a dashboard narrative (Cockpit); focuses on operating model.
8. **Auditability** — Decisions and triggers are explicit.
