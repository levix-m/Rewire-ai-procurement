# Evaluation criteria — Contract AI

1. **Citation accuracy** — Points to real sections user provided; no hallucinated clauses.
2. **Humility on law** — Clear Legal review prompts; no legal conclusions.
3. **Playbook alignment** — Non-standard flags match supplied standard definitions.
4. **Structure** — Clause map is scannable and reusable in CLM metadata.
5. **Consistency** — Amendments integrated without contradictions left silent.
6. **Privacy** — Minimal sensitive data in summaries.
7. **Differentiation** — Does not attempt invoice-line matching (Invoice Compliance agent).
8. **Operational utility** — Renewal and obligation lists are actionable.
