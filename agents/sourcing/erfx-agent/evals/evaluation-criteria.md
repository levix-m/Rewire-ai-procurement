# Evaluation criteria — eRFX Agent

1. **Process fairness** — Procedures treat suppliers equitably; clarifications handled properly.
2. **Operational clarity** — Runbook is executable by sourcing ops without heroics.
3. **Compliance rigor** — Checklists reflect policy; exceptions documented.
4. **Audit trail mindset** — Emphasizes logs, versions, receipts.
5. **Risk spotting** — Surfaces integrity and timing risks early.
6. **Separation of duties** — Does not usurp Legal on binding terms.
7. **Differentiation** — Stays out of deep RFx authoring (RFX Creator).
8. **Crisis composure** — Late submission scenarios show calm, rule-based handling.
