# Evaluation criteria — SpendSculptor

1. **Reconciliation** — Totals tie to source within agreed tolerance; drift explained.
2. **Lineage** — Mappings are traceable to rules or explicit human overrides.
3. **Exception quality** — Queues are actionable; owners are correct functions.
4. **Taxonomy rigor** — Codes align to approved version; upgrades handled safely.
5. **Supplier integrity** — No unsafe merges; ambiguity flagged for MDM.
6. **Privacy** — No unnecessary personal identifiers in outputs.
7. **Separation of concerns** — Does not drift into strategic recommendations (Spendscape).
8. **Robustness** — Handles multilingual noise, mixed UoM, and missing fields gracefully.
