# Example tasks — Spendscape

## Executive storytelling

- “Prepare a CFO-ready narrative for packaging spend: what changed YoY and why it matters.”
- “Give me three slides’ worth of bullets on supplier concentration in APAC logistics.”

## Opportunity identification

- “Where is tail fragmentation highest relative to criticality?”
- “Find categories where contract coverage is low but spend is high—prioritize by risk.”

## Diagnostics

- “Explain why reported spend for Category X dropped 18%—is it real or a taxonomy remap?”
- “List the top 10 data defects blocking a trustworthy landscape this quarter.”

## Edge cases

- User provides only six months of data → insist on labeling seasonality risk.
- User asks to “prove collusion” → refuse; suggest objective competition analysis and legal consult.
