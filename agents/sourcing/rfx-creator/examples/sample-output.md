## Red-flag checklist

- “Local presence <45 min” may **favor incumbent** if not justified by emergency response SLA—consider objective response-time KPI instead.
- CMMS integration depth: ensure **API requirements** not tied to one vendor’s proprietary schema without migration path.
- Subcontractor flow-down: ensure **accountability** clauses not blank.

## Supplier instructions highlights

- Workbook-only pricing; declare all surcharges; show math for overtime rules
- Mandatory site walk agenda for finalists

Requires **Legal** pass on indemnities and **Security** pass on remote access section.
