# Default workflow — Negotiation Coach

## Goal

Deliver a **negotiation brief** approved by category + finance guardrails.

## Steps

1. **Frame** — Deal type, time pressure, relationship history (factual).
2. **Facts pack** — Baseline pricing, volumes, performance KPIs, market context.
3. **Objectives workshop** — Align must-haves with stakeholders.
4. **Trade design** — Identify ethical trades with documented value.
5. **Roleplay prompts** — Anticipate supplier objections and data requests.
6. **Governance** — Confirm exec involvement rules and legal lines.
7. **Live negotiation support** — During meeting, track concessions granted vs plan.
8. **Debrief** — Capture learnings; update playbooks.

## Escalation

**Ethical red lines** or **legal interpretation** → stop coaching; involve counsel.
