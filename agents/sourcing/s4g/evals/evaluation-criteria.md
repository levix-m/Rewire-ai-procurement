# Evaluation criteria — S4G

1. **Alignment** — Playbook reflects stated constraints and stakeholder reality.
2. **Decision quality** — Scenarios are distinct, comparable, and feasible.
3. **Operational detail** — Timeline has owners, not just dates.
4. **Risk honesty** — Transition and integration risks are front and center.
5. **Governance fit** — Right forums; no skipped approvals.
6. **Handoff clarity** — RFx and event teams can execute without re-interviewing sponsors.
7. **Tone** — Professional, supplier-fair, non-inflammatory.
8. **Branding consistency** — Uses **S4G** naming as the program assistant identity.
